Who we are

Caring, experienced podiatry in the Inner West

Inner West Podiatry is a friendly local podiatry clinic based in Glebe, focused on helping people of all ages walk more comfortably. With experience across general foot care, biomechanics and long-term conditions, we take the time to listen, explain and build a plan that makes sense in real life.

Whether you're on your feet all day at work, chasing kids in the park, training for a run or managing a health condition like diabetes, we work with you to get your feet feeling as good as they can.

Personal, one-on-one care

Every appointment is one-on-one with your podiatrist, with time to go through your history, examine your feet and lower limbs, and explain what's going on in plain language. You'll leave knowing what the plan is, why it should help, and what you can do at home between visits.

Inner West Podiatry welcomes patients referred under Medicare chronic disease plans, Department of Veterans' Affairs, private health insurance and self-referred patients.

What we treat

Pain & overuse problems

  • Heel pain & plantar fasciitis
  • Arch pain and flat feet
  • Forefoot pain & Morton's neuroma
  • Shin splints & running injuries
  • Knee pain related to foot posture

Skin, nails & general care

  • Ingrown toenails (conservative & surgical options)
  • Corns, callus & cracked heels
  • Thickened or fungal nails
  • Plantar warts
  • Regular nail and skin care

Children, diabetes & long-term foot health

  • Children's walking patterns & growing feet
  • Diabetic foot checks & ulcer prevention
  • Footwear advice for school, sport & work
  • Custom and prefabricated orthoses
  • Home exercise & strengthening programs

You'll find more detail about specific conditions and how we treat them on our Conditions & treatments page.

Next steps

If your feet, heels or lower legs have been sore for a while – or you're just not sure what's going on – booking an assessment is a good first step. We'll talk through your story, examine how you stand and walk, and build a plan that fits your life, not just your feet.
