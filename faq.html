Fees & frequently asked questions

New to podiatry, or wondering how appointments work at Inner West Podiatry? Here's a quick overview of fees, referrals, rebates and what to expect.

Fees

Fee details can change over time, so for the most up-to-date information please call the clinic on (02) 8040 6687. As a general guide:

  • Initial consultation – longer appointment to take history and examine your feet and lower limbs.
  • Standard review consultation – for ongoing care and follow-up.
  • Extended or complex appointments – for more involved cases when extra time is needed.

We'll always let you know what any recommended treatment is likely to involve, including costs, before we go ahead.

Common questions

Do I need a referral?
No, you can make an appointment directly. If you have a chronic disease management plan from your GP, please bring the paperwork with you so we can process any eligible visits.
Can I claim through my private health insurance?
Many extras policies include podiatry. The exact rebate depends on your fund and level of cover. If you're unsure, it's a good idea to check with your health fund before your appointment.
What should I bring to my appointment?
It's helpful to bring:
  • Your usual shoes (work, casual and any sport shoes you use often).
  • A list of your medications and relevant medical history.
  • Any referral letters, imaging reports or care plans from your GP or other health professionals.
What happens at a first visit?
We'll talk through what's been happening, examine your feet and lower limbs, and often watch you stand and walk. There's time for questions, and we'll explain our findings in plain language before suggesting any treatment.
Do you see children?
Yes. Children's appointments are relaxed and age-appropriate. We're happy to see kids for concerns about walking, pain, school shoes or sports.
Do you offer home visits?
Home visits may be available in Glebe and nearby suburbs for patients who have difficulty getting to the clinic. Please call to discuss your situation and availability.

Still have a question?

If you're not sure whether we're the right fit for your needs, or you have a question that isn't answered here, please feel free to call the clinic on (02) 8040 6687. We're always happy to talk things through.
