Getting Here

We're easily accessible by public transport, with bus stops nearby on St Johns Road. Street parking is available, and we're just a short walk from Glebe Point Road's cafés and shops.

The clinic is located on the ground floor with wheelchair access available. If you have any accessibility requirements, please let us know when booking so we can ensure your visit is comfortable.

Parking Information

Street parking is available on St Johns Road and surrounding streets. Please check signage for time restrictions. Disability parking spaces are located nearby for those with permits.
